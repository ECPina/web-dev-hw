But why something EMO?

You must be wondering: "But why would you draw this?" Growing up, emo was the very hot trend. Everyone had raccoon eyes and work Tripp NYC® pants. And while at the time these kids were often seen as outcast, I found myself fitting right in with them. I never got to own my own pair of Tripp NYC®, BUT I did have a jacket.

But it was more than the clothes and boys in guyliner. It was the music I was really drawn to. This is were I found my love for everything punk, rock, post-hardcore, techno related. I spoke to me on a level that I had never really experienced before. So I spent hours listening to Senses Fail and doodling sad emo girls and writing even sadder stories (but don't worry; I grew up to be mostly functional).

TL;DR

Theres a sense of nostalgia there for me. I really enjoyed the culture, even if it was a bit silly. I thought it would be a fun way to express a part of myself.
