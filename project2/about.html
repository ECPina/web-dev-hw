But why something EMO?

You must be wondering: But why? As a teen, I found myself drawn to a subgroup of people: the Emo crowd. Everyone had raccoon eyes and wore Tripp NYC® pants. And while at the time these kids were often seen as outcast, I found myself fitting right in with them. And while I never got my own pair of Tripp NYC®, BUT I did have a jacket.

It was more than the clothes and boys in guyliner, though. It was the music I was really drawn to. This is were I found my love for everything punk, rock, and post-hardcore. There was something so incredibly relatable about the music. It's sad and angry, just as I was. So I spent hours listening to Senses Fail and doodling sad emo girls and writing even sadder stories (but don't worry; I grew up to be mostly functional).

TL;DR

Theres a sense of nostalgia there for me. I really enjoyed the culture, even if it could be a bit silly. I thought it would be a fun way to express a part of myself.
